Church Street Park Northern Lot Closure April 27-June 1

Morrisville's Church Street Park Northern Lot, located at 5800 Cricket Pitch Way, will be closed to the public from Monday, April 27, to Monday, June 1, while lighting upgrades are installed. The closure will impact the cricket field, pitch, walking track, shelter, grandstands, playground, tennis courts and parking lot.

Meet Your Town Council

 

The Town Council represents the voice of our residents—shaping policies, setting priorities, and making decisions that reflect the needs and values of our community. Through the council-manager form of government, the Council works closely with a professional Town Manager to ensure services are delivered efficiently and transparently.
