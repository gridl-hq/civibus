Meet the Durham City Council Members

Learn more about the current Durham City Council Members.

  • City Council has 7 members: the Mayor, 3 Council Members representing specific wards, and 3 at-large Council Members. 
  • Terms are staggered, with nonpartisan elections every 2 years.

  1. Mayor Leonardo Williams

    Leonardo Williams was elected to serve as Mayor in 2023.

  2. Javiera Caballero

    Javiera Caballero was appointed in 2018 and first elected in 2019, now serving a second full term as an At-Large Council Member.

  3. Nate Baker

    Nate Baker was elected in 2023 to serve as an At-Large Council Member.

  4. Shanetta Burris

    Shanetta Burris was elected in 2025 to serve as the Ward 2 Council Member.

  5. Chelsea Cook

    Chelsea Cook was appointed in 2024 and first elected in 2025 to serve as the Ward 3 Council Member.

  6. Matt Kopac

    Matt Kopac was elected in 2025 to serve as the Ward 1 Council Member.

  7. Carl Rist

    Carl Rist was elected in 2023 to serve as an At-Large Council Member.
