Contact the Board of Commissioners

The Board of Commissioners welcomes your feedback, questions, and comments.

 

To invite the Commissioners to local events or meetings, please email commissioners@dconc.gov or clerk@dconc.gov.

To communicate with all the Commissioners, please email commissioners@dconc.gov.

 

You may send mail to:

Durham, NC 27701
