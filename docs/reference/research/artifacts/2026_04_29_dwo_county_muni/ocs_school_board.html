Board of Education

The School Board is comprised of those community members elected to set policy and direction for the local school district. The Orange County Schools Board of Education includes seven members who serve four year terms.

Contacting the Board

Click here to sign up to receive email notifications about all regular and special called OCS School Board meetings. (This does not include ad hoc committee meetings.)

To contact the entire membership of the School Board at once, send an email to: ocsboardmembers@orange.k12.nc.us*

Leave a Message for a Board Member

  • Voicemails: For leaving a message for a Board Member, the public should call 919-732-1721.
  • Board Information (calendars, meeting agendas, website, etc.): Direct the public to contact Franklin.
  • Voicemail System Questions: If there are questions about how the voicemail system works, please contact Franklin.

You may also call or send a message to:
