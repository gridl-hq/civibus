About CHCCS Board of Education

The Chapel Hill-Carrboro City Schools Board of Education is comprised of seven community members elected to set policy and direction for the school district. Members of the Board serve staggered four-year terms. Expand the menu items on this page to see upcoming work sessions and regular meetings, committee meetings and public comments. Scroll down for contact information.

CHCCS Board's Mission Statement

To facilitate the CHCCS Mission by establishing and maintaining policies that are in the best interest of our students, communicating and engaging with key stakeholders, and hiring and collaborating with the district superintendent.

 

 

Contact CHCCS Board of Education

To send a message to all CHCCS Board Members and the Superintendent's Office, please email: allboardmembers@chccs.k12.nc.us

All mail correspondence to and from the Chapel Hill-Carrboro City Schools is subject to the North Carolina Public Records Law, which may result in disclosure to third parties.

You can expect to receive a response to your email from a board representative to ensure receipt.

To contact the Clerk to the Board of Education, Tamara Holloway, please email tholloway@chccs.k12.nc.us.
