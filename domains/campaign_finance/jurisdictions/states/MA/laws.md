# Massachusetts campaign-finance law notes

This file is narrative support for the MA `laws` block in `config.yaml`.

## Authoritative source

- OCPF Laws and Guidelines: https://www.ocpf.us/Legal/LawsAndGuidelines
- MA General Laws Chapter 55: Campaign Finance

## Key rules

- Individual contribution limit: $1,000 per candidate per calendar year (one of the strictest in the nation).
- PAC contribution limit: $1,000 per candidate per calendar year.
- Corporate direct contributions: prohibited.
- Union direct contributions: prohibited.
- Itemization threshold: $50.
- Ballot question committees have different rules and limits.
- Electronic filing is required for all committees.
