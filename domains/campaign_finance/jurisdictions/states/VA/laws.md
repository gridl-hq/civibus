# Virginia (VA) — Campaign Finance Laws Reference

## Governing authority

Virginia Department of Elections (ELECT)
`https://www.elections.virginia.gov/`

## Contribution limits

Virginia has **no limits** on contributions to candidates or political committees from any source.

- Individual to candidate: **unlimited**
- PAC to candidate: **unlimited**
- Corporate direct to candidate: **allowed, unlimited**
- Union direct to candidate: **allowed, unlimited**
- Party to candidate: **unlimited**

## Itemization threshold

Contributions of **$100 or more** must be itemized with donor name, address, occupation, and employer.

## Reporting

- Candidate campaign committees file periodic disclosure reports
- Report types include: regular periodic reports, large contribution reports ($500+ within 24 hours of certain deadlines), final reports
- Electronic filing is required for committees raising or spending $25,000+
- Filing deadlines vary by election cycle and office level

## Key statutes

- Virginia Code Title 24.2, Chapter 9.3 — Campaign Finance Disclosure Act
- `https://law.lis.virginia.gov/vacodefull/title24.2/chapter9.3/`

## Notes

- Virginia is one of a handful of states with no contribution limits
- The lack of limits means the primary value is transparency/disclosure rather than enforcement of caps
