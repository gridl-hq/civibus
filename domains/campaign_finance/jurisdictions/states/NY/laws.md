# New York campaign-finance law notes

This file is narrative support for the NY `laws` block in `config.yaml`.

## Authoritative source

- NY Election Law Article 14: https://www.nysenate.gov/legislation/laws/ELN/14-100
- NY State Board of Elections: https://www.elections.ny.gov/

## Key rules

- Corporate contributions are prohibited (NY Election Law 14-116).
- LLC contributions are treated as individual contributions.
- Contribution limits vary by office level (governor vs. legislature vs. local).
- Itemization threshold: $99.
- Electronic filing is required for all committees.
- NY has a separate public campaign finance matching program (PCFB) for state elections.
